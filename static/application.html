Travel

In this section we will ask you some questions regarding your travel to Košice.

Travel reimbursement info

  • If you come from Slovakia, we sadly can not give you travel reimbursement.
  • If you come from neighbouring countries (i. e. Czechia, Poland, Ukraine, Hungary or Austria), you may receive travel reimbursement up to 20€.
  • If you don't come from countries listed above, you may receive travel reimbursement up to 50€.

Criteria for recieving reimbursement:

  • You must apply for reimbursement in your application.
  • You must arrive and check in at the event.
  • You must demo a project on the event.
  • You must provide a proof of travel expences (i. e. invoice or reciept documenting travel-related expenses).

Reimbursement will be paid out afrer the event concludes. You can find the status of your reimbursement in your dashboard. If you've met all the criteria, we'll contact you by email about your reimbursement.
